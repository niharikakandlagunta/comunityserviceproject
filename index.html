📖 About the Project

Understanding access to safe drinking water through community surveys.

Safe drinking water is essential for human health and daily life. This project aims to study water availability, identify challenges, and create awareness about water purification and hygiene practices.

⚠️ Problem Statement

🚰 Water Contamination

Unsafe water may contain pollutants and harmful microorganisms.

🧼 Poor Sanitation

Poor sanitation affects water quality and public health.

🦠 Water-Borne Diseases

Contaminated water can cause infections and diseases.

🎯 Objectives

  • ✔ Study the availability of safe drinking water.
  • ✔ Identify sources of drinking water.
  • ✔ Understand challenges faced by people.
  • ✔ Analyze awareness about water purification methods.
  • ✔ Examine health issues caused by unsafe water.
  • ✔ Suggest improvements for access to safe drinking water.

🧼 Hygiene

Maintain cleanliness and sanitation.

🌱 Save Water

Avoid water wastage.

✅ Conclusion

Safe drinking water is essential for a healthy society. This project helped understand water accessibility, awareness levels, and challenges faced by the community while promoting safe water practices.
